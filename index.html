About Us

Mark & Angie Gray, owners of 'MGBD Parts,' bring over 30 years of experience in maintaining and restoring Rover P6 cars, spanning various models and conditions. They've channeled their expertise into 'MGBD Parts,' a business dedicated to providing quality parts for Rover P6 variants produced between 1963-1977. Their inventory includes new, NOS (New Old Stock), refurbished, and second-hand parts. If you can't find what you need on their webstore, they offer technical advice and assistance. The webstore, active since July 2012, continually expands, and they provide competitive shipping rates. They also attend UK shows and events for in-person part collection. Registered users receive updates on new parts and offers through the shop's newsletter.
